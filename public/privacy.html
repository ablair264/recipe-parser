Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 5, 2025

Controller: Splitfin Ltd

This Privacy Policy explains how Get The Recipe (the “App”) collects, uses, and protects your information. This policy applies to our iOS app and accompanying web service and is operated by Splitfin Ltd (“we”, “our”, or “us”).

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (required for account creation and login)
  • Password (if you sign up with email and password; stored in hashed form by our authentication provider)
  • Google account basic profile (if you choose “Continue with Google”: name, email, and profile image)

App Data

  • Recipes and related content you save in the App
  • Basic device and app information needed to operate the service (e.g., version, platform)

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you and secure your account
  • Sync and display your saved recipes across your devices
  • Provide customer support and respond to requests
  • Maintain and improve App functionality and reliability

Sign In Methods

You can sign in using either email and password or your Google account. When using Google, we receive only your basic profile information (name, email, and profile image) for authentication. We do not access your Google Drive or any other Google data.

Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication and database services are provided by Supabase.
  • Hosting and content delivery are provided by Netlify.
  • Passwords (for email sign‑in) are stored only in hashed form by our authentication provider; we never store plain‑text passwords.
  • We apply industry‑standard safeguards to protect your data in transit and at rest.

Service Providers (Processors)

  • Supabase – Authentication and database services
  • Netlify – Web hosting and CDN

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share data with service providers who help us operate the App (e.g., authentication, hosting). They are bound by contractual obligations to protect your data and use it only for the services we request.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users’ safety.

Retention

We retain your account information and saved recipes for as long as your account remains active. If you request deletion of your account, we will delete your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your account details within the App.
  • Delete Account: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. See Contact below.
  • Email Preferences: We may send essential service emails (e.g., security, account notices). We do not send marketing emails without consent.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and adjust the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the revised policy.

Contact
